Lasagna

A simple lasagna recipe that will satisfy everyone. This simple multilayered dinner is simple and quick to make, yet so delicious

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Spaghetti Sauce
  • Cheeses(cottage cheese, mozarella, parmesan)
  • Eggs
  • Seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Water

Steps to make Lasagna

  1. Cook and drain the ground beef, then stir in the spaghetti sauce and simmer.
  2. Combine the cottage cheese, 2 cups of mozarella, eggs, half of the Parmesan, and seasonings.
  3. Assemble the lasagna according to the detailed recipe.
  4. Bake, covered, for 45 minutes.
  5. Uncover and continue baking for 10 minutes
