Spaghetti

Have you ever been able to taste the delicious combination of meatballs and noodles. Spaghetti is just that delicious tomato sauce combined with meaty goodness and cheese. What more could you ask for.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti sauce
  • Noodles
  • Meatballs
  • Mozarella

Steps to make Spaghetti

  1. Boil noodles: Boil noodles according to your package
  2. Cook Meatballs: Cook meatballs on medium-high heat until they are brown on the outside
  3. Combine: Combine tomato sauce, noodles, meatballs, and alittle bit of pasta water until warm.
  4. Plate: Plate and add shredded mozarella to garnish.
